what?

this contest consists on a small competition to see what drawing will make the face of our cups for the next year

how?

all you have to do is send the drawing (in a PNG or JPEG file) to our email with the following subject: MY CUP OF ART_NAME. (ps. change the section NAME to your actual name).

rules

besides the email you'll receive, we will post the top 3 results in our website and Instagram.


all drawings and images submitted are for the solo purpose of this contest and this contest only!
