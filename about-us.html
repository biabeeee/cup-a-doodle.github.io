'cup-a-doodle' is a small and innovative coffee shop with an unique approach.

with a '60s cartoon style mixed with a more modern architecture, this coffee shop brings a fun and relaxed environment.

allowing its costumers to put their creativity and imagination in a cup, this coffee shop is also an experience that you don't want to miss ;)

with the rush of everyday life, we barely look around, and it becomes harder to appreciate the beauty of the world we live in. we want to change that.

in 'cup-a-doodle' the costumer can unwind and dedicate some time to a craft lost in between work and chores. 
cup-a-doodle - you may surprise yourself!

created and found by Charlotte and her group of professional friends that in January 2024, this project is something that they want to see grow with them.

your FAQ

can I study in your space?

cup-a-coffee is a space build were comfort is prioritized. we have the perfect chairs and cushions to put your study to date!
(PS. we have plugs strategically placed near this places :))

can I take my 'doodled-cup' home?

of course!! depending on the type of cup you have an extra fee may be added.

why don't you have wi-fi on sundays?

we focus on moments. sundays are for most people the day before week of stress, by disabling the wi-fi we are hoping people disconnect from the stresses of the internet and focus on relaxing and enjoying that moment.

what kind of supplies do you have?

usually we have pencils, markers, crayons and acrylic paint of different colors. if they run out during the day we'll only replace them in the next day.
